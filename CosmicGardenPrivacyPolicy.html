隱私權政策

生效日期： 2026 年 1 月 7 日

Cosmic Garden（以下簡稱「本應用程式」、「我們」）非常重視您的隱私權。本隱私權政策旨在說明我們如何收集、使用、披露及保護您的個人資訊。當您安裝或使用本應用程式時，即代表您同意本政策所述之內容。

1. 我們收集的資訊

為了提供完整的服務功能並優化使用者體驗，我們可能會要求您授權並收集以下特定資訊：

A. 位置資訊 (GPS)

  • 收集內容： 我們會收集您的精確位置或粗略位置資訊（GPS 及網路定位）。
  • 使用目的： 此資訊用於提供本應用程式的核心功能（例如：地圖導航、定位標記、周邊服務搜尋等）。我們僅在應用程式使用期間存取此資訊，除非您明確允許背景存取。

B. 網路連線資訊 (Wifi)

  • 收集內容： 我們會偵測您的 Wifi 連線狀態、SSID（網路名稱）及訊號強度。
  • 使用目的： 用於確認網路連線品質以確保資料傳輸順暢，或用於特定硬體連線功能。

C. 設備資訊

  • 手機型號 (Device Model)： 我們會收集您的設備製造商、型號及作業系統版本。這有助於我們分析應用程式在不同設備上的相容性，並快速排除技術故障（Debug）。
  • 電池電量 (Battery Level)： 我們會讀取您的設備電池狀態與電量百分比。這用於優化應用程式的效能管理（例如：在低電量時自動切換至省電模式），以避免過度消耗您的電力。

2. 我們如何使用您的資訊

我們收集的資料僅用於以下用途：

  • 提供、維護及改善本應用程式的服務功能。
  • 分析使用趨勢，以優化使用者介面與體驗。
  • 偵測、預防及解決技術問題或資安漏洞。

3. 資訊的分享與揭露

除以下情況外，我們不會將您的個人資訊分享給第三方：

  • 法律要求： 為了遵守法律程序、法院命令或政府機關的要求。

4. 資料安全

我們致力於保護您的資訊安全，並採取合理的技術與管理措施來防止資料遺失、遭竊或未經授權的存取。然而，請注意，網際網路傳輸無法保證 100% 安全。

5. 您的權利

依據適用法律，您擁有以下權利：

  • 隨時透過手機系統設定，撤銷對位置資訊或 Wifi 的存取權限。
  • 要求查詢、更正或刪除我們所收集關於您的個人資料。

6. 隱私權政策的修訂

我們可能會不時更新本隱私權政策。任何變更將發布於本頁面，並更新上方的「生效日期」。建議您定期查看本政策以獲取最新資訊。

7. 聯絡我們

如果您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問或建議，請透過以下方式聯繫我們：

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 07, 2026

Cosmic Garden ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services and improve user experience, we may request access to and collect the following specific information:

A. Location Information (GPS)

  • What we collect: We collect your precise or approximate location information (via GPS and network-based positioning).
  • Purpose: This information is used to enable core features of the App (e.g., map navigation, location tagging, searching for nearby services). We only access this information while the App is in use, unless you explicitly grant background permission.

B. Network Connection Information (Wi-Fi)

  • What we collect: We detect your Wi-Fi connection status, SSID (network name), and signal strength.
  • Purpose: This is used to verify network quality to ensure smooth data transmission or for specific hardware connectivity features.

C. Device Information

  • Device Model: We collect information about your device manufacturer, model, and operating system version. This helps us analyze App compatibility across different devices and quickly troubleshoot technical issues (debugging).
  • Battery Level: We read your device's battery status and percentage. This is used to optimize the App's performance management (e.g., automatically switching to power-saving mode when the battery is low) to avoid excessive power consumption.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionalities.
  • To analyze usage trends and optimize the user interface and experience.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security vulnerabilities.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal processes, court orders, or governmental requests.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase, AdMob) to assist with data analysis or advertising. These parties access necessary data only to perform specific tasks and are obligated to keep it confidential.

4. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Revoke access permissions for Location or Wi-Fi at any time via your device settings.
  • Request to access, correct, or delete the personal data we have collected about you.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
